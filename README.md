# GenLayer Project Showcase

## From MVP to Growth Milestones

GenLayer is dedicated to fostering innovation and growth within its ecosystem. Our journey from Minimum Viable Product (MVP) to significant growth milestones is powered by a community-centric approach, emphasizing rewarding builders and amplifying their achievements.

## Early Ecosystem Grants: Build & Earn

We believe in empowering our early contributors. Through our **Early Ecosystem Grants** program, developers and innovators can:

*   **Earn Rewards as You Build:** Contribute to the GenLayer ecosystem and receive grants for your development efforts, fostering a sustainable and vibrant community.
*   **Get Amplification through GenLayer's Channels:** Achieve significant milestones with your project, and we'll help amplify your success through our official channels, reaching a broader audience and attracting further support.

Join us in building the future of GenLayer!
